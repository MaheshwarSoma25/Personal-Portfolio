PROJECTS


Innovative Congestion Control System for Ambulance
An intelligent traffic control system using RFID and Arduino to prioritize ambulances at intersections. Automatically switches signals to green when an ambulance is detected, ensuring fast and safe passage.
Tech Stack: RFID, Arduino Uno, Embedded C, LCD, Traffic Module
Team Members: P. Maheshwari, MD. Sana Thaharin, SK. Samrin, S. Maheshwar
Institution: Sri Venkateswara Engineering College, Suryapet
Guide: Mrs. B. Saritha, Assistant Professor, ECE Dept.
Bus Detection Device for Visually Impaired Individuals
A portable RFID-based system that helps visually impaired individuals identify bus stops and available buses through voice output. Enhances independence and safety in public transportation.
Tech Stack: RFID Reader, Arduino Uno, Voice IC, LCD, Speaker
Team Members: P. Maheshwari, MD. Sana Thaharin, SK. Samrin, S. Maheshwar
Institution: Sri Venkateswara Engineering College, Suryapet
Guide: Mrs. B. Saritha, Assistant Professor, ECE Dept.
